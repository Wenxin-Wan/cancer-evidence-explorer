What's linked to cancer?

An interactive map of the exposures scientists have linked to different cancers, from the World Health Organization's IARC Monographs. Start with a cancer, start with an exposure, or explore the whole network.

💛
Please read. This shows whether something can cause cancer in general (a population-level “hazard”) — not what caused any individual's cancer, and not your personal risk, which also depends on dose, duration, genetics and chance. Most people with these exposures never get cancer. For your own health, talk to a doctor.
